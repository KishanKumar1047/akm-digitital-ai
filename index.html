How We Use AI

The AI Infrastructure Behind Every Engagement

Search Evolution

The Three Tracks of AI-Era Search

Search behavior is undergoing a structural transformation. Brands that adapt now will own dominant visibility for 2025–2030.

AIO

AI Overview Optimization

Google's AI Overviews now answer queries directly — reducing click-through rates for traditional SEO. AIO ensures your brand is cited inside these AI-generated answer boxes.

  • Direct question-answer format in the first 100 words of every page
  • Explicit entity naming and cross-linking
  • FAQ schema markup on all key pages
  • Topical authority architecture to signal expertise to Google
GEO

Generative Engine Optimization

Optimizing content to appear in AI-generated answers from ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini. A new competency no traditional agency owns at scale.

  • Authoritative, data-backed, source-referenced definitive guides
  • Citation tracking via Perplexity searches and brand mention tools
  • Structured content that AI tools quote as primary sources
  • E-E-A-T signals woven into every content asset
AEO

Answer Engine Optimization

Structuring content for voice search, featured snippets, and zero-click results — requiring technical precision that generalist agencies lack.

  • FAQ, How-To, and Speakable schema implementation
  • Optimized H2 structure and paragraph length for snippet eligibility
  • Meta answer optimization for zero-click visibility
  • Voice query targeting for Alexa, Siri, and Google Assistant
